INTRODUCTION

Welcome to 'You bleed just to know you're alive,' a fansite for Peko Pekoyama. I host all my analysis of her character here, and you can navigate through the site by clicking the links on the right. If you want to talk to me about my analysis, you can visit my contact page. As for why I created this small little site, I felt like no one had really analyzed Peko all that much, and I wanted to present my thoughts on her as a whole. And here we are. Peko's wasted potential, and I think she deserves something nice instead being treated and treating herself as a tool the whole series.

In the brief time when she was in Danganronpa, she existed as a stoic woman with a calm, cool, and collected exterior. She's shown to be emotionally closed off, as seen in her free time events when Hajime teaches her how to smile without forcing it. This is one of the few times we see her true emotions, aside from before her death where she asks to be remembered and for Fuyuhiko to be forgiven. Underneath all those layers, I think she dehumanizes herself as some sort of coping mechanism, albeit not a healthy one. There's a lot of ways in which Peko dehumanizes herself, and I have reasons for why I think she does this. I'll explain this further in my analysis.



PROFILE

Full Name: Peko Pekoyama. Her name reflects a Japanese onomatopoeia - pekopeko. The sound refers to bowing - a common practice in Japanese culture when meeting someone. Simply, it’s a symbol of politeness.

Ultimate: The Ultimate Swordswoman.

Age & Birthdate: Said to be in her early twenties, late teens. Her age is unclear. However, we do know that she was born on June 30th, in the mid to late 1990’s.

Zodiac: Cancer. This is ironic, considering that Cancers are considered to be emotional.

Blood Type: O

Height: 5’6 (172cm)

Weight: 112 lbs (51 kg)

Bust: 85cm

Likes: The color black, and she seemed to develop a romantic interest in Fuyuhiko. In her free time events, she mentions that she liked animals, specifically that she likes to pet their soft, fluffy fur.

Dislikes: Candy.
